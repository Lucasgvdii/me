NumiaAI

Active Built at Numia

Overview

Domain expert chatbot developed to provide support for the trading, reporting and strategy teams of blockchain foundations. Currently used by the DYDX foundation.

The Problem

Blockchain foundation teams need quick access to complex on-chain data and insights, but querying databases and interpreting results requires technical expertise. This creates bottlenecks for trading, reporting, and strategy teams who need data-driven answers fast.

The Solution

NumiaAI is an intelligent chatbot trained on blockchain domain knowledge and connected to real-time data sources. Teams can ask questions in natural language and receive accurate, contextual answers about their protocol's performance, market conditions, and strategic metrics.

Key Features

  • Natural language query interface
  • Real-time on-chain data access
  • Domain-specific knowledge base
  • Trading and market insights
  • Custom report generation
