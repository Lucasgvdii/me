CloudClerk

Active Cofounder - CEO

Overview

BigQuery cost monitoring and optimization platform. We leverage over 5 years of experience of big data processing in BigQuery to help teams reduce costs through proper observability tools plus the usage of AI Agents.

The Problem

Organizations using BigQuery often struggle with unexpected costs and lack visibility into their query spending. Without proper monitoring, teams can easily overspend on data processing without realizing the impact until the monthly bill arrives.

The Solution

CloudClerk provides real-time cost monitoring, query optimization recommendations, and AI-powered agents that automatically identify and suggest improvements for expensive queries. Our platform gives teams full visibility into their BigQuery spending and helps them optimize their data workflows.

Key Features

  • Real-time cost monitoring and alerts
  • Query performance analysis
  • AI-powered optimization recommendations
  • Team spending dashboards
  • Historical cost trends and forecasting
