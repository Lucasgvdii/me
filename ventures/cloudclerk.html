CloudClerk

Visit Website Live
Co-Founder - CEO

Understand and Reduce Your BigQuery Costs with the Right Tools

CloudClerk aims to provide the most powerful and transparent tooling for organizations to understand, control, and reduce their BigQuery costs, returning full ownership of cost intelligence to the teams that generate and consume data.

Unlike traditional approaches that rely on one-off audits or black-box recommendations, CloudClerk is built to empower customers throughout the entire optimization journey. The platform equips companies with everything they need to investigate, analyze, and optimize their BigQuery billing autonomously, while being continuously supported with expert guidance.

Platform Capabilities

  • Simplicity - Intuitive tools that make finding and understanding cost issues easy
  • Transparency - Full visibility into why costs are incurred, not just where
  • AI Powered - Automatic issue detection, smart monitoring, and tailored recommendations from our AI agents
  • Ownership - Self-serve platform that returns full control of cost intelligence to your team

What CloudClerk Delivers

  • Full-featured platform for BigQuery cost analysis and optimization
  • Consulting and advisory services for complex or large-scale environments
  • Audits and cost diagnostics - Always paired with tooling and knowledge transfer
  • In-house and privacy-first deployments for organizations with strict data governance

This hybrid approach ensures that teams are not left with static reports, but with capabilities they can own and extend over time.

Impact

  • Up to 40% reduction in BigQuery costs for enterprise customers
  • Immediate improvements in cost predictability and accountability across data teams
  • Faster iteration cycles due to clearer visibility into performance–cost trade-offs

How It Started

CloudClerk emerged from more than five years of hands-on experience processing petabytes of data in BigQuery at scale.

The founding team brings deep expertise in BigQuery internals, billing mechanics, and large-scale data workloads, developed through years of operating high-volume production systems. This experience enabled the team to understand not only how BigQuery is used, but also how its billing system can be optimized, tuned, and — when needed — strategically re-architected.

CloudClerk was built to solve a recurring problem seen across data-driven organizations: rapidly growing BigQuery costs paired with limited visibility and control. Instead of offering superficial fixes, the team set out to build a solution that combines advanced tooling, deep technical knowledge, and long-term customer enablement.

Today, CloudClerk stands as a trusted optimization layer for BigQuery, helping organizations align their data ambitions with sustainable, predictable cloud costs.
