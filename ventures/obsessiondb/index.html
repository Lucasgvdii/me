ObsessionDB

Visit Website Live
Founding Engineer — Cloud Infra/Systems & Lead FDE

Performance Per Dollar, Redefined

ObsessionDB is pushing the limits of what hosted ClickHouse® databases can deliver per dollar. Built for teams that need serious analytical power without the typical cloud markup, ObsessionDB offers optimized infrastructure designed to maximize query performance while keeping costs predictable and low.

Why ObsessionDB

  • Cost efficiency - Significantly lower price point than major cloud-hosted ClickHouse® providers
  • Performance first - Infrastructure tuned specifically for ClickHouse® workloads
  • No compromise - Full ClickHouse® compatibility without feature locks or artificial limitations
  • Simplicity - Managed hosting that removes the operational burden of running ClickHouse® yourself

The Compute-Compute-Storage Separation Layer

At the core of ObsessionDB sits a proprietary compute-compute-storage separation layer designed to push managed ClickHouse® performance per dollar beyond what standard hosted offerings deliver. Users can scale storage, ingestion compute, and query compute independently — adapting each cluster to its exact workload rather than buying into pre-defined "instance sizes".

The Ecosystem

  • Obsession Console — A console layer that fills the gaps in the native ClickHouse® experience: materialized views backfilling, jobs management, settings modularity, and analytics. Open at console.obsessiondb.com.
  • CH-KIT — The first open-source drizzle-kit-style tooling for ClickHouse®, available in TypeScript and Python. Designed to enable serious-scale adoption by giving developers the schema management and migration workflows they expect from modern databases. Available at chkit.obsessiondb.com.
  • Forward Deployed Engineering — Hands-on architectural support embedded directly with customer teams, for organizations where in-house ClickHouse® expertise is scarce.

How It Started

ObsessionDB was born from years of running ClickHouse® at scale across data-intensive products. After repeatedly hitting the ceiling of what existing hosted solutions could offer — both in performance and in cost — the team decided to build something better.

The result is an infrastructure layer obsessively optimized for ClickHouse®, stripping away unnecessary overhead and passing the savings directly to customers.
