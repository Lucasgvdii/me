Founding Engineer — Lead FDE, Data & Product

The First Blockchain Data Cloud Platform

Numia is the first data cloud platform purpose-built for institutions, blockchains, and developers, covering the full on-chain data lifecycle — from core infrastructure to advanced analytics, activation, and growth.

End-to-End Services

  • Critical blockchain infrastructure - High-performance, low-latency RPC and LCD nodes used in production to interact with and query blockchains in real time
  • Large-scale data ingestion - Processing full blockchain state and continuously parsing, normalizing, and structuring raw on-chain data
  • Advanced data processing and analytics - Transforming raw blockchain data into reliable, actionable insights
  • Product and data access layer - Advanced dashboards, real-time APIs with complex metrics, and business intelligence services
  • Growth and data-driven marketing - Address-level attribution, on-chain user behavior analysis, and optimization of adoption strategies

Numia enables protocols, developers, and institutions to operate, understand, and scale blockchain systems at an enterprise level, turning on-chain data into a durable competitive advantage.

Numia by the Numbers

  • 100+ petabytes of on-chain data processed
  • 50+ blockchains indexed, spanning multiple ecosystems
  • 30+ blockchain foundations and core protocol teams as paying clients
  • Hundreds of million requests powering blockchain frontends with over 99.9% uptime for teams like Osmosis or dYdX

Numia is a primary data provider for leading global blockchain analytics platforms, including Dune, Token Terminal, Nansen, Blockpit, and Artemis — platforms whose data is consumed by institutional investors, financial terminals, enterprises, and public-sector entities, including integrations with Bloomberg and government-level analytics environments.

In addition, Numia powers or feeds data into widely used market tools such as CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap, DeFiLlama, and other industry-standard platforms.

How It Started

Numia originated from a founding team with deep expertise in blockchain architecture, large-scale data engineering, distributed systems, and production-grade protocols.

From the beginning, the team focused on solving one of the ecosystem's hardest problems: processing and extracting value from massive volumes of on-chain data. Prior experience designing, deploying, and operating mission-critical infrastructure enabled the creation of a platform capable of handling large, complex, and continuously growing datasets, while maintaining enterprise-level reliability and performance.

While Numia's roots are closely connected to the Cosmos ecosystem, its architecture has always been chain-agnostic and built for scale, applying best-in-class data engineering and cloud infrastructure principles across blockchains and distributed systems.

Today, Numia combines deep blockchain expertise with a strong specialization in large-scale data processing, positioning itself as a strategic partner for any organization that needs to operate and derive insight from on-chain data at scale.
