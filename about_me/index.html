About me

A bit of who I am

Hey! I'm Lucas, co-founder of CloudClerk, Numia, ObsessionDB, and Convea. I'm deeply driven by data and complex systems, and I spend most of my time turning one into solutions for the other. I'm a workaholic by nature, which helps when your brain never stops looking for the next problem worth solving.

I'm originally from Spain (Alcalá de Henares), but I've lived and worked across Europe — from a forest outside Helsinki (Finland) to Florence (Italy) and Cannes (France). I operate fluently in Spanish, English, and French.

I'm competitive by design. I hold a red belt in taekwondo (one step away from black, by choice rather than ability), reached the top 2% of League of Legends players worldwide, and now focus that same intensity on bouldering. I also love surfing, though I don't do it as often as I'd like to.

Although now I'm too obsessed with building projects, I've read obsessively. My favorite genre has always been fiction (to name a few of my long-time favorite authors: Dumas, Emilio Salgari, Laura Gallego, and Patrick Rothfuss), although there are many socio-economics-related titles that I'm passionate about.

I really enjoy debating — about anything interesting, from philosophical topics to politics, economics, or even fruit rankings. That aren't many things I enjoy as much as exchanging opinions on topics that aren't simple or don't have a clear answer.

I'm a Data Architect and Entrepreneur with a focus on building data products that solve real problems at scale. Currently, I lead CloudClerk as CEO, where we're building BigQuery cost monitoring and optimization tools — combining 5+ years of big data experience with AI agents to help teams gain observability and reduce costs.

In parallel, I'm co-founder and Data Lead at Numia, processing petabytes of real-time blockchain data. Our analytics products are used by major foundations including DYDX, Celestia, and Cosmoshub. I've also co-founded ObsessionDB, pushing the performance-per-dollar limits of hosted ClickHouse® databases.

I hold an EIT Digital Master's in Data Science and Entrepreneurship from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid and Aalto University (Finland), graduating with a 9.2 GPA, and a Bachelor's in Computer Science Engineering.

Across my ventures, I've helped generate over $2M in revenue, reached more than 150,000 individual users, and served over 50 client businesses. I believe in building products that are technically excellent and genuinely useful — no shortcuts, no fluff.
