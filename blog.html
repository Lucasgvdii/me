Scaffolding AI

A practitioner's guide to building AI that works in production

AI is more and more embedded in how we work, build, and think every day. Rather than treating it as a black box, we try to understand its mechanics, adapt to where it's heading, and push it as far as it can go. This blog is a record of that path — the concepts we learn, the patterns we find, and the lessons worth sharing for anyone on a similar journey.
